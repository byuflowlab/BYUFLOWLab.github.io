Project Presentation

Your final deliverable consists of two parts: 1) preparing and delivering a presentation summarizing your project, 2) watching 12 other projects and providing peer reviews.

You will record a 6-minute max presentation and make it available to the class (via an online spreadsheet we will make available). The presentation will be peer reviewed based on the following criteria:

  • Engineering (20%): Is this an engineering problem or does it use physics/engineering-based deep learning methods?
  • Quality (30%): Is the work of good quality and technical accurate? Do they use appropriate architectures and best practices?
  • Scope (30%): Is the problem of reasonable scope (2-3 homework equivalent effort)?
  • Presentation (20%): Is the presentation clear, well-organized, visually interesting?
