Critical Design Review and Midterm 2

Your homework for this week is to prepare for the design review, prepare for the midterm, and take the midterm.

Critical Design Review

Same format as last time. The main focus will be on providing feedback and suggestions on your built airplane and any flight testing to date. Some elements I recommend preparing include:

  • Your airplane (bring it with you). Discuss challenges, areas of improvement, and any questions you have.
  • Video(s) of the aircraft in flight. Discuss observed performance from flight testing.
  • Telemetry testing and any questions you have.
  • Any in-flight performance data.
  • Schedule/plan for the rest of the semester.

Midterm 2

Same format as last time. Take-home via Learning Suite, open book/notes/calculator, 2 hour time limit, focused on fundamental concepts. The exam will be available on Learning Suite starting after class on Wednesday and must be completed by midnight on Tuesday (11/20/2018). You can take it anytime you wish during that interval, but must do so in one sitting.

The exam will focus on the topics since the last midterm (propulsion, performance, structures, and a little bit of control), however it is comprehensive.
