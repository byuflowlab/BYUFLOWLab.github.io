HW 3: Wing Design

due 10/4/2017 before midnight via Learning Suite 50 possible points

  1. Design an appropriate wing for your project. Make sure the lift distribution provides a high inviscid span efficiency, and the cl distribution provides favorable stall characteristics. Make sure that is adequate margin between your design CL and the wing CLmax (or equivalently between your design speed and stall speed).
