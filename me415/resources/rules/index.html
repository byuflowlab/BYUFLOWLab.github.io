Lab Rules and Safety

Respect the Lab

  • Receive training before using foam cutters and battery chargers
  • Use a mat when cutting or gluing
  • Keep construction projects away from computer stations
  • Never take tools from the lab (use the checkout room)
  • No flying in the lab, including glide tests
  • Put tools away and clean up after yourself
  • Label projects that must be left in common workspace areas using provided labels

Respect Others Projects

  • Keep to your own storage area (contact lab manager if you need more room)
  • Do not touch or handle projects you are not involved in
  • Never prop the door open

Respect the Safety of Yourself and Others

  • Safety glasses are required when you or anyone near you is using tools
  • Never supply a motor with electricity when a propeller is attached
  • Thrust tests are allowed when everyone is behind the propeller
  • Never climb on tables (use the ladder)
  • Exercise proper handling of batteries (the EE shop will dispose batteries for you)
  • No painting
  • Most composite work should be done in the composites lab

NOTE: The lab is equipped with an eye wash station. Desired training is available upon request.
